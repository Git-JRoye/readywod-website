10 Minute Beginner Series

Complete setup guide to get started with your year-long beginner fitness program

About the 10 Minute Beginner Series

The 10 Minute Beginner Series is a comprehensive year-long program designed to help you build a consistent fitness routine. This program is perfect for beginners and can be customized to fit your schedule.

  • Program Duration: 1 year (52 weeks)
  • Default Schedule: 5 days per week
  • Workout Length: 10 minutes per session
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose your workout days (3, 4, or 5 days per week)

Note: If you select fewer than 5 workout days per week, the program will automatically extend to ensure you complete all workouts.

2
Configure Your Workout Days
Screenshot: Program Schedule screen showing the "Use Custom Schedule" toggle and workout days selection

Set Your Schedule

In the Program Schedule settings, you'll configure which days of the week you want to work out.

  1. Enable the "Use Custom Schedule" toggle at the top
  2. Select your preferred workout days by tapping the checkboxes next to each day:
    • Monday through Friday (recommended for 5 days/week)
    • Or choose 3-4 days that work best for your schedule
  3. You'll see a summary showing which days are selected (e.g., "5 selected")
  4. The schedule preview will display: "Your workouts will be scheduled on: [selected days]"
  5. Tap "Save" in the top right corner to save your preferences
  6. Close the settings by tapping "Cancel" or the back button

üìÖ Important: The program will only schedule workouts on the days you select. If you choose 3 days per week instead of 5, the program will last longer than a year to ensure you complete all workouts.

3
Open the Menu and Select the Program

Navigate to Programs

Now that your schedule is configured, it's time to load the 10 Minute Beginner Series program.

  1. From the main workout screen, tap the hamburger menu button (‚ò∞) in the upper left corner
  2. The side menu will slide in from the left
  3. In the menu, locate and tap "Programs"
  4. Expand "10 Minute Series" by tapping on it
  5. Select "Beginner" from the list

üí° Tip: The menu shows your current track (e.g., "Beginner Track") at the top, and all available programs are organized by category for easy navigation.

4
Select Your Start Date
Screenshot: Date picker or calendar interface for selecting the start date

Choose When to Begin

After selecting the Beginner program, the app will prompt you to choose a start date for your program.

  1. A date picker or calendar will appear
  2. Select the date you want to begin the program
  3. This will be the date of your first workout
  4. Confirm your selection

üìÖ Scheduling Logic: The first workout will load on your selected start date. Subsequent workouts will follow in order, but will only appear on the workout days you configured in Step 2. For example, if you selected Monday-Friday and start on a Wednesday, your next workout will be on Thursday, then Friday, then Monday (skipping the weekend).

5
Program Loaded and Ready

Your Program is Active

Once you've selected your start date, the 10 Minute Beginner Series program will be loaded and ready to use!

  1. The first workout will appear in the Normal View on your selected start date
  2. You can view upcoming workouts in advance using:
    • The calendar widget at the top of the workout screen
    • The Schedule tab in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Workouts will automatically appear on your selected workout days
  4. Each workout includes:
    • Warm-up exercises
    • Main 10-minute workout
    • Recovery/stretching recommendations
