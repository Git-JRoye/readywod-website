What is ReadyWOD?

The Problem We Solve

Every training session begins with the same frustration — too many choices. Over the years, fitness enthusiasts collect thousands of workouts across spreadsheets, screenshots, and notes, but accessing them quickly and intelligently remains a challenge.

ReadyWOD eliminates this decision fatigue by providing one-tap workout generation with the flexibility to modify movements or reps, all while working completely offline.

Our Solution

ReadyWOD is a fully offline, AI-driven iOS fitness app that combines years of real workout data with Core ML intelligence. Built using AI-assisted development, it learns from your workout history to recommend balanced, personalized routines.

The app features smart movement rotation, custom workout creation, and comprehensive logging — all designed to make training effortless and intelligent.

Key Features

  • 🧠 Core ML Integration: Learns from user history to recommend balanced workouts and prevent overtraining.
  • 🔄 Smart Movement Rotation: Ensures variation and balanced muscle group targeting.
  • 🏋️ Custom Workouts: Add your own sessions with automatic warm-up and recovery generation.
  • 🧾 Workout Logging: Tracks all sessions locally or via iCloud sync.
  • ⏱️ Built-in Timers: EMOM, AMRAP, and lifting percentage calculators included.
  • 💪 Bodybuilding Programs: Dedicated hypertrophy routines and training modes.
  • 🌐 100% Offline: Everything runs on device with no internet dependency.
  • ☁️ iCloud & StoreKit: Seamless syncing and premium subscription management.

🌐 Privacy-First Design

All workouts, recommendations, and user data are stored on your device. There are no servers, no trackers, and no dependencies — just fast, private fitness intelligence that works anywhere, anytime.

🤖 Built with AI, Designed for Humans

ReadyWOD was created using AI-assisted development with Cursor, proving that non-developers can build production-level apps with the right tools and determination. The result is an app that combines the best of human insight with artificial intelligence.
