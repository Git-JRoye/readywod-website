Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. ReadyWOD is designed with privacy-first principles.

Effective Date: March 2nd, 2025

ðŸ”’ Privacy-First Design

ReadyWOD does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app operates completely offline without tracking, logging, or analytics.

What We Don't Collect

ReadyWOD is designed with privacy as a core principle. We don't collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information
  • Workout data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Any other identifying information

How ReadyWOD Protects Your Privacy

ReadyWOD operates entirely on your device. All workout data, preferences, and history are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your workout data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is controlled entirely by you and Apple's privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

ReadyWOD does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app is completely self-contained.
